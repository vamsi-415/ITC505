How to Deliver a Specific Font to a Client

One popular method to deliver a font to a client is by using Google Fonts. This involves the following steps:

  1. Choose a Font: Visit Google Fonts and select a font that is not likely to be present on most people's computers.
  2. Embed the Font: Click on the selected font, then click on "Select this style". Copy the link tag under the "Embed" section.
  3. Add the Link to Your HTML: Paste the link inside the <head> section of your HTML file.
  4. Use the Font in Your CSS: Apply the font to your CSS by using the font-family property.

This method is simple and efficient as it leverages Google's servers to deliver the font, ensuring fast load times and easy implementation.
