Lamborghini Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is a super sport utility vehicle (SUV) that combines the performance of a sports car with the practicality of an SUV. It has a sporty, elegant, and off-road design and is designed to handle a variety of terrains, including sand, ice, gravel, and rocks. The Urus has a 657-hp twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8 engine, permanent all-wheel drive, and an eight-speed automatic transmission. It also has a classic Lambo cabin with jet-fighter design cues, faux-suede and carbon-fiber trim, a fully digital gauge cluster, and two touchscreens built into the center stack
